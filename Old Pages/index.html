Business Convergence

Business Convergence is an Information Technology (IT) Consulting company that specializes in Enterprise Applicaiton Integration (EAI).

While integration is our focus,  we have found that most our customers are looking for a complete IT solution.  Therefore we also provide custom software development, the supporting technical and business process documentation, and training to support the life-cycle of the solution.

 


 

Our flagship integration platform, ConX, is built build on an open architechture that has a primary function of connecting both proprietary (on-premis) and cloud based applications.  

 

ConX is built on an open architecture that removes the need for extensisive integration coding.  ConX allows an administrative user of an application ot log into the aplication using Conx, and conX will display the applications database.   Once the database is exposed, the end-user can "ddrag and drop" any fields of the source data into a target applciation or report.
