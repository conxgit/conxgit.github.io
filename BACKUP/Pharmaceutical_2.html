Regulatory Compliance

Business Convergence is deeply rooted in the pharmaceutical industry and has extensive experience with Computer System Validation (CSV). President, Mike Burns led the IT and Automation departments for both Glaxo Smith Kline (GSK) and Pfizer pharmaceuticals prior to starting Business Convergence. While at GSK Mike formed the CSV department based on GAMP 4 and later implemented GAMP 5 while at Pfizer.

The duties while at the manufacturing facilites of GSK and Pfizer included: Maintaining clean room (Pressure, Temperature, and Humidity), automating a partical counting application (Wonderware), maintaining the pressure separation of a containment facility within the plant, replacing a Building Automation system, replacing a Token Ring network, and replacing all serviers, routers and desktops and the introduction and validation of virtual servers.

Lastly, Mike led the retirement effort of all computer systems and their associated data prior the plant closure.
